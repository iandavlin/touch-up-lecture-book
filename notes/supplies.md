# Touch-Up Optical System — Lecture Supplies

This document lists physical materials required for live demonstrations.

---

# 1. Lighting

## 1.1 Controlled Light Sources
- 2700K warm incandescent bulb (true tungsten preferred)
- 5000K–6500K daylight LED (high CRI preferred, CRI 90+)
- Adjustable desk lamp (single-source control)
- Extension cord / power strip

## 1.2 Optional (Advanced Demonstrations)
- Narrow-spectrum red LED flashlight
- Neutral gray reference card
- Lux meter (optional, for controlled comparisons)

---

# 2. Color Demonstration Materials

## 2.1 Paint Swatches (Home Depot or similar)
- Warm beige / tan
- Saturated red
- Saturated blue
- Muted red (low chroma)
- Ultra pure white (reference)


# 3. Film & Path-Length Demonstrations

- Clear acrylic (plexiglass) sheet, ~1/8"
- Optional second acrylic sheet (for stacking thickness demo)
- Spray bottle (water mist for wetting demonstration)
- Microfiber cloth

---

# 4. Wood Samples

- Figured maple sample
- Straight-grain hardwood sample
- End-grain block
- Sealed vs unsealed comparison boards
- Dyed wood sample
- Filled pore sample (pigment-based)
- Filled pore sample (dye-based oil + pumice)

---

# 5. Pore & Geometry Demonstration

- Wood sample with open pores (e.g., mahogany, ash)
- Sample with filled pores
- Dark filler sample
- Natural filler sample

---

# 6. Surface Geometry Demonstration

- Gloss sample board
- Satin sample board
- Matte sample board

---

# 7. Miscellaneous

- Painter’s tape
- Sharpie marker
- Ruler
- Small flashlight
- Camera or phone (for documenting angle shifts)
